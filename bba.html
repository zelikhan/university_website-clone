Introduction

The Air University Kamra, Department of Business Administration (DBA)offers students a variety of specialization in Aviation Management, Finance, Marketing, Project Management and Human Resource Management.The curricula of the degree programs are fully aligned with the requirements of the accreditation bodies to comprise state-of-the-art courses that will prepare students for future challenges in computing and related fields. The department strives to maintain high standards through quality in teaching, learning, and research, with an emphasis on theoretical foundations, practical skills and cutting-edge applications of the subjects. To enhance and groom the soft skills of students, co-curricular and extra-curricular activities are planned and organized throughout the degree program. Our programs prepare students with a foundation in the major functional areas within business, such as accounting, economics, entrepreneurship, finance, information systems, international business, management, marketing and small business as well as Aviation Industry.
