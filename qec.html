AAUCK QEC

Welcome to the Office of Research, Innovation and Commercialization (ORIC), established in 2021 at Air University, Aerospace and Aviation Campus Kamra, as per guidelines and directives of HEC – Higher Education Commission, to ensure the research a top priority at AU, Kamra Campus and to promote the research-intensive culture. ORIC at AUAACK is striving to bridge the gap between the academia and industries for joint research projects and deals with pertinent activities to promote the research, innovation, commercialization and Industrial collaboration, startups and entrepreneurship, trainings / seminars and workshops. ORIC facilitates Research and Development (R&D) activities at Kamra Campus and offers research-oriented conducive environment where our researchers can interact, work and develop research-based technologies. AUAACK aims to become a leader in R& D and provide consultancy services to the industries.
